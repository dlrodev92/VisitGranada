Your Guide to Visiting Granada

Planning your journey to Granada? Whether you’re looking for travel tips, top accommodations, or hidden gems, we’ve got you covered. Start your adventure on the right foot with all the essential visitor information.

"Give him alms, woman, for there is nothing in life as sorrowful as being blind in Granada." – Francisco de Icaza

Travel Reminders

  • Check travel documents (passport, ID) and ensure they're valid.
  • Book accommodations in advance, especially during peak season.
  • Bring comfortable walking shoes; Granada has many cobbled streets.
  • Download offline maps or ensure internet access for navigation.
  • Consider booking tickets for major attractions, like the Alhambra, beforehand. They might take months of queues to buy them
