The Heart of Granada: Los Cármenes Stadium

Los Cármenes Stadium is not just an arena; it’s a symbol of pride and unity for the city of Granada. With a capacity of over 19,000 seats, it becomes a sea of red and white as fans gather to support Granada CF. Whether you're a lifelong supporter or a visitor, the energy here is unforgettable.

Every match day, fans pour into the stadium to cheer for Granada CF, creating an atmosphere that reverberates through the city. It’s an experience that embodies the passion of Andalusian football.

Originally opened in 1995, Los Cármenes has hosted countless memorable matches, from thrilling local derbies to historic international games. Its walls have seen the rise of legends and the fervor of fans, making it a true football landmark.

The Legacy and Spirit of Granada CF

Founded in 1931, Granada CF has come a long way, evolving from a regional club to a competitive team in Spain’s top leagues. The club’s legacy is rich with stories of resilience, perseverance, and passion that inspire fans worldwide.

Granada CF’s journey has been marked by both challenges and triumphs, with each season bringing new milestones and unforgettable memories for fans and players alike.

The spirit of Granada CF is rooted in the community, with fans who proudly support their team through thick and thin. The club’s achievements on the field are a testament to the dedication and determination of both players and supporters.
